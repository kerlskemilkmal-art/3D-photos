About the Exhibition

Welcome to 3D by kero, a digital gallery showcasing high-resolution artistic scenes generated through generative AI and rendered using standard WebGL technologies.

By arranging image planes in mathematical coordinates (cylindrical, helical, and cartesian), this workspace combines classic 2D art with three-dimensional space.

Technologies used:

  • Three.js for WebGL 3D rendering and shading
  • GSAP (GreenSock) for high-performance camera animations
  • OrbitControls for intuitive touch/mouse orbital physics
  • Vanilla HTML5, CSS3 Custom Properties & flexbox grid layouts
